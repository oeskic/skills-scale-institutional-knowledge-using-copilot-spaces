# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Collaborate with **Technical Lead** on architecture and code quality standards
- Work with **QA Engineers** on test coverage and acceptance criteria verification
- Coordinate with **Project Manager** on schedule and blockers
- Receive requirements from **Business Analyst** and **Product Manager**

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partner with **Project Manager** on delivery timelines and scope
- Engage with **Business Analyst** on requirements clarification
- Coordinate with **Sponsor** on strategic priorities
- Support **Developers** with prioritization context

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partner with **Product Manager** on scope and priorities
- Support **Scrum Master** on process governance and escalations
- Coordinate with **Sponsor** and stakeholders on executive updates
- Work with **Technical Lead** on timeline feasibility
- Track blockers identified by all team members

---

## QA / Quality Assurance Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality by developing comprehensive test strategies, validating acceptance criteria, and identifying risks before release. They collaborate across the team to build confidence in deliverables.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and execute test plans aligned with acceptance criteria
- Identify and track defects and quality risks
- Collaborate with developers on test automation strategy and coverage
- Validate release readiness before production deployment
- Support regression testing and smoke test execution
- Define testing best practices and quality metrics

### Goals
- Ensure features meet acceptance criteria before release
- Build test automation to reduce manual effort and cycle time
- Identify quality risks early and help mitigate them
- Maintain high customer satisfaction through quality

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and quality reports
- Defect tracking and severity assessments
- Collaboration on acceptance criteria refinement
- Release readiness sign-offs

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partner with **Developers** on test strategy and automation
- Validate **Product Manager** acceptance criteria
- Collaborate with **Technical Lead** on testing architecture
- Report quality metrics to **Project Manager**
- Support **Scrum Master** in maintaining quality standards
- Escalate blockers to **Project Manager** and **Sponsor** if needed

---

## Scrum Master / Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies and coaching. They remove impediments, foster continuous improvement, and help teams optimize velocity and process health.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives
- Help the team identify and resolve impediments blocking progress
- Track sprint velocity and burndown
- Coach teams on agile practices and continuous improvement
- Remove organizational and process blockers to team autonomy
- Mentor team members on collaboration and self-organization

### Goals
- Enable consistent, predictable delivery cadence
- Improve team velocity and efficiency
- Foster psychological safety and continuous learning
- Reduce cycle time and remove waste from processes

### Typical Communication
- Agile ceremony facilitation and notes
- Retrospective action items and follow-ups
- Coaching conversations on process improvement
- Burndown charts and velocity metrics

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Work with **Project Manager** on milestone planning and risk escalation
- Coach **Developers** on estimation and sprint discipline
- Support all team members in removing impediments
- Partner with **Technical Lead** on maintaining quality in sprints
- Escalate persistent blockers to **Project Manager** and **Sponsor**

---

## Technical Lead / Solutions Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide technical direction, design system architecture, and mentor the development team. They balance innovation with maintainability and ensure scalable, robust solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Design system architecture and define technical approach
- Provide technical guidance through code review and design mentoring
- Identify and mitigate technical risks and technical debt
- Balance feature velocity with code quality and maintainability
- Mentor junior developers and share technical knowledge
- Make technical trade-off decisions in collaboration with Product and Project Managers

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable, high-quality code
- Reduce technical debt and improve system reliability
- Mentor team members to improve overall technical capability
- Enable fast, predictable delivery

### Typical Communication
- Architecture design docs and decision logs
- Technical guidance in code reviews and design discussions
- Technical risk assessments
- Mentoring and knowledge-sharing sessions

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Collaborate with **Developers** on design and code quality
- Work with **QA** on testing strategy and automation architecture
- Partner with **Project Manager** on technical feasibility and timeline
- Support **Product Manager** with technical trade-off analysis
- Escalate technical risks to **Project Manager** and **Sponsor**

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between stakeholder needs and technical implementation. They gather, clarify, and document requirements to ensure solutions deliver business value.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and clarify business requirements from stakeholders
- Document acceptance criteria and business rules
- Validate solution fit against business objectives
- Identify and escalate scope changes and dependencies
- Support change management and stakeholder communication
- Act as subject matter expert on business processes and rules

### Goals
- Ensure solutions align with business needs and strategy
- Reduce scope creep and change requests through clear upfront requirements
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction through communication and validation
- Enable faster, more accurate estimation and delivery

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and user stories
- Acceptance criteria refinement sessions
- Change request assessment and impact analysis
- Stakeholder communication and expectation management

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partner with **Product Manager** on requirements prioritization
- Collaborate with **Developers** on acceptance criteria clarity
- Work with **Technical Lead** on technical feasibility
- Support **Project Manager** in tracking scope and dependencies
- Engage **Sponsor** and stakeholders on requirements validation
- Update **QA** on business rules and test scenarios

---

## Sponsor / Executive Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Sponsors provide executive oversight, strategic alignment, and resource support. They make high-level decisions and remove organizational blockers critical to project success.

### Responsibilities
- Provide executive oversight and strategic alignment
- Approve project charter, budget, and major scope changes
- Make escalation decisions and remove high-level blockers
- Monitor business value delivery and ROI
- Communicate project status to broader organization
- Ensure resources and priorities align with organizational strategy

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers strategic business value
- Remove organizational blockers to enable team success
- Maintain executive visibility into project health
- Manage stakeholder expectations and communication

### Typical Communication
- Executive status updates and business case reviews
- Escalation decision-making
- Budget and resource allocation approvals
- Strategic alignment and organizational communication

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Receive regular updates from **Project Manager** and **Product Manager**
- Make final escalation decisions on risks and blockers
- Provide strategic context and priority guidance to team
- Approve major scope or timeline changes recommended by **Project Manager**

---

## Security / DevSecOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Security Engineers ensure that security and compliance requirements are integrated throughout the development process. They conduct threat modeling, security testing, and lead incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Review designs and code for security vulnerabilities
- Conduct security testing and threat modeling
- Ensure compliance with security policies and standards
- Define and maintain security scanning in CI/CD pipelines
- Lead incident response for security-related issues
- Advise on secure architecture and best practices

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities from reaching production
- Maintain compliance with security policies and regulations
- Enable secure delivery without slowing time to market
- Foster security awareness across the organization

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews and threat models
- Vulnerability reports and remediation guidance
- CI/CD security scanning and testing results
- Security incident response and post-mortems
- Security training and awareness sessions

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Review **Technical Lead** architecture for security risks
- Conduct security testing alongside **QA Engineers**
- Provide guidance to **Developers** on secure coding
- Report security risks to **Project Manager** and **Sponsor** for escalation
- Partner with **DevOps** on infrastructure security
- Support **Product Manager** in assessing security impact of features

- The interaction sections show which roles typically collaborate and in what context.
- When multiple personas are involved in a project, refer to the interaction guidance to understand communication flows and dependencies.
